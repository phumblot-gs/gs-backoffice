# Methods Officer

The Methods Officer is the planning and coordination agent that receives a goal from the CEO, breaks it down into scoped steps each carrying its own acceptance criteria, and delegates those steps one by one to the Engineer by opening child issues; it then reviews the Engineer's output—the diff together with its accompanying report—against the criteria, iterating with further instructions whenever the work falls short, and once every step's criteria are met it opens the pull request that brings the completed change up for human review.
